Welcome to Signal Resort

Signal resort, a home inside home!. We are one of the best resort located in Lagos Nigeria. With over 50acres of nature filled environment, come and experience warm African hospitality, comfort and luxury. Our packages offer a mix of tradititonal architecture and furnishings with all modern armenities you would expect in a 5-star resort. And if you prefer the great outdoors, we provide a range of camping options and other sport activites such as volleyball, mini-golf, tennis, soccer, basketball, swimming and many more.
We offer tasty African dishes presented with a continental flair or international cuisine served with an African twist. Be sure to have a sip of our fresh palmwine or coconut water anytime you visit!. Explore, play, bond with families and team members, go on picnic, fishing, kayaking, quad biking or do nothing but relax in between the warm golden sand on the beach - whatever rocks your boat.
The beautiful ambience of Signal Resort is ideal for camping, picnic, luxury vacation, honeymoon, wedding, conferences, corporate retreat and team bonding.
